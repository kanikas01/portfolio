About Me

Hi there, thanks for stopping by! My name is Kurt Statham, and I'm a budding young web developer. I spent over 12 years in the casual games industry doing technical production on sites like Addicting Games and Shockwave. Now that I've moved on from that I'm hoping to pivot to more of an engineering role.

Originally from Oklahoma, I got my B.A. in French from UC Berkeley in 2003. I also hold a certificate in Java Programming from City College of San Francisco. Prior to working in casual games I had careers as both a bartender and a musician. I currently reside in Los Angeles, California, and when I'm not coding websites you can find me at the dance studio around the corner from my apartment where I study (and occasionally teach) various styles of ballroom dance.
